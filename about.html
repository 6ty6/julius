ABOUT US

An International
Oil Marketing Company

Pure Heights Energy Trading FZCO is incorporated under the laws of the United Arab Emirates as a wholly owned Ghanaian company, which specializes in the importation and distribution of refined petroleum products to the African market. As one of the reliable suppliers of petroleum products in Africa, Pure Heights Energy Trading FZCO trades in a wide range of petroleum products including Gasoline, Gasoil and Jet A-1.

Our mission is heading towards a destination of excellence in fuel solutions.
